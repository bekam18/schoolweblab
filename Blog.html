About us

Madda Walabu University is a public university located in Robe, Bale Zone, Oromia Region, Ethiopia. It was established in 2002 and offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various fields of study. The university is known for its focus on research and community engagement, as well as its commitment to providing quality education to its students. Madda Walabu University aims to contribute to the development of the region and the country as a whole through its academic programs and research initiatives.
